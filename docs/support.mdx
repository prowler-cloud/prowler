## Lighthouse AI

Lighthouse AI can answer many questions about your security posture, findings, compliance status, and remediation guidance directly within Prowler.

Available in Prowler Cloud and Prowler App.

[Learn more about Lighthouse AI](/user-guide/tutorials/prowler-app-lighthouse)

## Support Desk

> Available to **Prowler Cloud** customers.

For Prowler Cloud customers, submit support requests through our support desk. We'll route your request to the right team and respond via email.
