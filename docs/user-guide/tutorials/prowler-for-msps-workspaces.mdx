A workspace groups customers inside your partner organization. Workspaces are how you scope what each team member can reach: a member is invited into a workspace and sees the customers in that workspace, unless their role grants access to all of them.

<Warning>
**A workspace belongs to you, not to your customer.** It is a container on your side of the relationship. Your customer's own environment is their Prowler Cloud tenant, reached through the [Customers](/user-guide/tutorials/prowler-for-msps-customers) page.
</Warning>

## When Workspaces Matter

If your whole team works across every customer, you can ignore workspaces entirely — everything lands in the default workspace and Superadmins reach all of it.

Workspaces earn their keep when you need separation: a team per region, per practice, or per customer segment, where an Organization Admin should only see their own book of business.

## The Default Workspace

Every partner organization is created with one workspace named **Default**, flagged as such on its card. It cannot be deleted. Customers you add without choosing otherwise land here.

## Reaching the Page

The Workspaces page lives at `/workspaces`.

<Note>
**Workspaces is not currently linked from the sidebar.** Navigate to `/workspaces` directly. Managing workspaces requires a role with **Manage workspaces**, which today means Superadmin.
</Note>

## The Workspaces View

Workspaces are shown as a grid of cards. Each card carries the workspace name, an **Active** badge, a **Default** badge on the default workspace, a four-slot stat area for **Orgs**, **Users**, **Accounts** and **Resources**, and the creation date.

## Create a Workspace

Click **Create Workspace** on the toolbar, give it a **Name**, and confirm. Names must be unique within your partner organization.

## Rename a Workspace

Open the card menu and choose **Edit Settings**. Only the name can be changed; the default flag is not editable.

## Delete a Workspace

Open the card menu and choose **Delete**. Deletion cannot be undone, and two rules block it:

| Condition | Result |
|---|---|
| The workspace is the **Default** one | Cannot delete the default workspace. |
| The workspace still holds customers | Cannot delete a workspace with assigned organizations. |

Move or remove the customers first, then delete.

## Workspaces and Team Access

Team access is granted per workspace, through the invitation that brings a member in:

* A **Superadmin** carries **Access all workspaces** and reaches every workspace without an explicit grant.
* An **Organization Admin** reaches only the workspaces they were granted.

See [Managing Your Team](/user-guide/tutorials/prowler-for-msps-team) for the full permission matrix.
