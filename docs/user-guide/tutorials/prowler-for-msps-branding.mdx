Upload your company logo to have it displayed alongside Prowler branding in the console. Branding is managed from **Settings → Branding** and requires a role with **Manage settings**.

## Upload a Logo

Open **Settings → Branding**, click **Upload**, and pick your file. The logo replaces the placeholder immediately and a confirmation appears.

{/* IMAGE (pending): /images/prowler-for-msps/settings-branding.png — Settings Branding tab showing the Partner Logo section with an uploaded logo, Upload and Remove controls */}

### Logo Requirements

| Requirement | Value |
|---|---|
| **Formats** | PNG or SVG |
| **Maximum file size** | 512 KB |
| **Maximum dimensions** | 200 × 60 pixels |

<Warning>
The upload dialog accepts files up to 1 MB, but the server rejects anything above **512 KB**. Keep your logo under 512 KB to avoid an upload that appears to start and then fails.
</Warning>

A wide, horizontal logo with a transparent background renders best. SVG stays crisp at every size and is the better choice where you have it.

<Note>
Uploaded SVG files are sanitized on the server. Scripts, external references and other active content are stripped before the file is stored.
</Note>

## Replace or Remove a Logo

Uploading a new file replaces the current one. **Remove** deletes it and returns the console to the default Prowler branding. Both take effect immediately for everyone on your team.

## Where Your Logo Appears

Your logo is shown alongside Prowler branding within the console for your team.

<Warning>
**Branding does not extend to Prowler Cloud.** Customers signing in at [cloud.prowler.com](https://cloud.prowler.com) see the standard Prowler interface. White-labeling Prowler Cloud is not supported.
</Warning>
